## Term 1
### Lesson 6 - Styles and Themes (Transitions, Animations, Design)
#### Recall
Students shall be asked to recall:
- Where to find copyright-free images online
- What image manipulation techniques they have already used in their "About Me" PowerPoint

#### Objective
Students shall know where to locate the options for styles, themes, transitions and animations, understand the effect of applying these tools to a PowerPoint, and practise applying styles and themes to their "About Me" presentation.

#### Styles and Themes
Students shall be taught:
- Where to locate the Design, Transitions and Animations tabs in PowerPoint
- The effect that applying a theme, a slide transition, or an animation has on a presentation, and when each is (and is not) appropriate

Students shall then practise the skill of applying a consistent theme, transitions and simple animations to their "About Me" PowerPoint, checking that their choices support rather than distract from their content.

##### Equipment used
###### Teacher
The teacher shall use the assigned PowerPoint to introduce the Design, Transitions and Animations tabs and model applying each.
The teacher shall use the screen to demonstrate the effect of overusing versus appropriately using animation and transitions.
###### Student
Each student shall use PowerPoint on their own PC to apply a theme, transitions and animations to their own "About Me" presentation.
