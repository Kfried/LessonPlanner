## Term 1
### Lesson 9 - Automating Your PowerPoint and Final Improvements
#### Recall
Students shall be asked to recall:
- What a URL is
- The difference between an internal and an external URL

#### Objective
Students shall know that PowerPoints can be automated, understand how to apply the correct settings for a timed, self-running presentation, and practise the skill of creating an automated PowerPoint, using the lesson to make final improvements to their "About Me" presentation.

#### Automating Your PowerPoint and Final Improvements
Students shall be taught:
- That a PowerPoint can be set to advance automatically, without a presenter clicking through it
- How to apply the correct timing and rehearse-timings settings to create a self-running, automated presentation

Students shall then practise the skill of automating their "About Me" PowerPoint, setting appropriate slide timings, and shall use any remaining time to review their presentation against the assessment criteria from previous lessons and make final improvements (checking spelling, image quality, and that all required elements - mind-map-based structure, manipulated images, styles/theme, sound/video, and URLs - are present).

##### Equipment used
###### Teacher
The teacher shall use the assigned PowerPoint to explain automation settings and model rehearsing and setting slide timings.
The teacher shall use the screen to demonstrate a fully automated presentation running in slideshow mode.
###### Student
Each student shall use PowerPoint on their own PC to set timings, automate their "About Me" presentation, and make final improvements.
