## Term 1
### Lesson 2 - NGRT Reading Assessment
#### Objective
Students shall complete the New Group Reading Test (NGRT) under formal test conditions, providing a standardised baseline measure of reading ability to inform teaching and support across the course.
#### NGRT Reading Assessment
Students shall be briefed on the purpose of the test and the formal test conditions before completing the assessment:
- Log in to the NGRT assessment platform using their own account
- Work silently and independently for the duration of the test
- Read each question carefully before selecting an answer
- Raise a hand rather than speaking if they have a technical issue
- Remain seated and continue working until instructed to stop, or move on to a quiet activity if they finish early

This is a standardised, timed assessment. The teacher does not teach new subject content this lesson, but does explain the purpose of the test and invigilates throughout.

##### Equipment used
###### Teacher
The teacher shall use a short briefing slide to explain the purpose and rules of the test.
The teacher shall use the NGRT administration platform to launch the test for the class and invigilate for the duration.
###### Student
Each student shall use their own PC, logged in with their own account, to access and complete the NGRT test online.
