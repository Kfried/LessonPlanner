## Term 1
### Lesson 7 - Adding Sound and Video
#### Recall
Students shall be asked to recall:
- Where to find the Design, Transitions and Animations tabs
- What effect a theme, transition or animation has on a presentation

#### Objective
Students shall know where to locate the options for sound and video, understand the different audio and video options available within PowerPoint, and practise the skill of adding video and audio content to their "About Me" presentation.

#### Adding Sound and Video
Students shall be taught:
- Where to locate the options for inserting audio and video in PowerPoint
- The different audio and video options available (for example: inserting a video file, recording narration, inserting an audio clip)

Students shall then practise the skill of adding appropriate sound and/or video content to their "About Me" PowerPoint.

##### Equipment used
###### Teacher
The teacher shall use the assigned PowerPoint to introduce the audio and video insertion options and model adding each.
The teacher shall use the screen, with speakers/headphones available, to demonstrate playback of inserted audio and video.
###### Student
Each student shall use PowerPoint on their own PC, with headphones where available, to add sound and/or video to their "About Me" presentation.
