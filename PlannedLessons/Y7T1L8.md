## Term 1
### Lesson 8 - URLs (Internal and External)
#### Recall
Students shall be asked to recall:
- Where to find the options for inserting audio and video
- The different audio and video options available in PowerPoint

#### Objective
Students shall know what a URL is and how to embed one in a PowerPoint, understand the difference between an internal URL (linking to another slide) and an external URL (linking to a website), and practise the skill of adding URLs to their "About Me" presentation.

#### URLs (Internal and External)
Students shall be taught:
- What a URL is and how to embed a hyperlink in PowerPoint
- The difference between an internal URL (a link that jumps to another slide within the same presentation) and an external URL (a link that opens a website)

Students shall then practise the skill of adding both an internal URL (for example, a link back to a contents slide) and an external URL (for example, a link to a website relevant to one of their hobbies) within their "About Me" PowerPoint.

##### Equipment used
###### Teacher
The teacher shall use the assigned PowerPoint to explain URLs and model inserting both an internal and an external link.
The teacher shall use the screen to demonstrate testing a hyperlink in slideshow mode.
###### Student
Each student shall use PowerPoint on their own PC, with internet access, to add an internal and an external URL to their "About Me" presentation.
