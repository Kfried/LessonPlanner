## Term 1
### Lesson 5 - Adding Images and Image Manipulation
#### Recall
Students shall be asked to recall:
- Why we use PowerPoint
- Why we use outline view
- Some of the icons and tools available in the PowerPoint ribbon

#### Objective
Students shall know how to find copyright-free images online for use in their own files, understand the range of image manipulation techniques available in PowerPoint, and practise manipulating images and adding them to their "About Me" PowerPoint.

#### Adding Images and Image Manipulation
Students shall be taught:
- How to find and collect copyright-free images online for use in other files
- The variety of image manipulation techniques available in PowerPoint (for example: crop, resize, colour and style effects, alt text)

Students shall then practise the skill of manipulating images and inserting them into their "About Me" PowerPoint, adding at least one appropriately manipulated, copyright-free image to each slide built from their outline.

##### Equipment used
###### Teacher
The teacher shall use the assigned PowerPoint to explain copyright-free image sources and model image manipulation techniques.
The teacher shall use the screen to demonstrate cropping, resizing and styling an image live.
###### Student
Each student shall use PowerPoint and a web browser on their own PC to source copyright-free images and add them to their "About Me" presentation.
