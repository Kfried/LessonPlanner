# Year 7 Term 1 #
## Term objectives ##
This term we shall begin with introducing the digital skills needed to work in the computer science lessons before moving on to the first topic area we want to teach, mind maps. Followed by working with Microsoft Powerpoint. The golden thread for this learning journey will be based around an "About me" theme where the students will build a mind map of basic information about themselves which they will use to develop a powerpoint about themselves.
